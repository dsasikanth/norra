# Norra — Knowledge Base

The single source of truth for what Nora (the AI assistant) tells prospects. Used in the live
Telugu agent's prompt (`india/cloud/bot.py`), and reusable for the English/Retell agent, the
website FAQ, and sales scripts. Works in any language — the agent answers in the caller's language.

## What Norra is
Norra is an **AI receptionist for local businesses**. It answers the phone like a warm, professional
front-desk person — but it's AI, so it never misses a call, never has a bad day, and works 24/7.
It books appointments, captures leads, answers common questions, and speaks the caller's language.

## Who it's for
Clinics, dental offices, physiotherapy & wellness centres, diagnostic labs, salons & spas, repair
shops, and other local businesses that take a lot of calls — especially where calls get missed
during busy hours, lunch, or after closing.

## What Norra does (features)
- Answers **every call, 24/7** — no busy signal, no voicemail, no missed calls.
- **Books appointments** straight into the business's calendar.
- **Captures every lead** — name, number, reason for calling.
- **Missed-call follow-up** — messages back callers it couldn't reach (WhatsApp in India, SMS in Canada).
- **Call summaries** — sends the owner a clear summary of every call.
- **Answers FAQs** — hours, location, services, prices.
- **Transfers to a human** or takes a detailed message when needed.

## Languages
Telugu, Hindi, English, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam and more — detects and follows the caller's language automatically.

## How it works / setup
Fully **done-for-you** — the business does no technical work. We learn their business (hours,
