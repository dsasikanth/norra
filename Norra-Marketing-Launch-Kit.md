## 1. Accounts to set up (priority order)

### A. Google Business Profile  ← do this first
Free, and the biggest credibility + local-search win. When someone Googles "Norra" or
"AI receptionist Milton," you show up with reviews, hours, and your site.
- google.com/business → create.
- Name: **Norra**
- Category: **Telephone answering service** (secondary: *Software company*)
- Service-area business (you go to clients / online) — set your service area (Milton/Halton, GTA).
- Add website, phone (647-360-3026 or a business line), hours, logo, and the description below.
- Ask your first happy pilot for a **Google review** — gold.

### B. LinkedIn Company Page  ← most important for B2B
- linkedin.com/company/setup/new
- Name: **Norra** · Tagline + About below · Industry: *Software Development* · Logo + banner (provided).
- Then post the launch announcement from your **personal** profile and tag the page.

### C. Instagram (Business account)
- Handle `@norrahq` · Bio below · Link norrahq.com · Profile pic (avatar provided).
- Switch to a **Business/Professional** account (Settings → Account type).

### D. Facebook Page (optional, good for local)

### E. Your personal LinkedIn
- Update headline: *"Founder at Norra · AI receptionist for local business"*.

## 3. First 2 weeks of content (so feeds aren't empty)

Post 2–3x/week. Keep the brand kit colours/voice.

1. **Launch announcement** — the story/post graphic + "Excited to share what I've been building…"
2. **Meet Norra** — the persona: "A receptionist with a human touch, powered by AI."
3. **The problem** — "Most small businesses miss 1 in 4 calls. Here's what that costs."
4. **How it works** — 3 steps (forward your line → Norra answers & books → you get a summary).
5. **Hear it / demo** — a short screen-recording or "DM me to hear Norra answer a call."
6. **Who it's for** — clinics, salons, trades, restaurants.
7. **FAQ** — "Is it really AI? Will it replace my receptionist?" (answer: it backs them up / is your front desk).
8. **Social proof** — after pilot #1, a testimonial. (Most powerful — save a slot for it.)

**Engagement:** join Milton/Halton small-business Facebook groups, comment helpfully,
and tag the Milton Chamber when relevant. Reshare anything the Chamber posts.
