# Norra — First Pilot Outreach Kit

Goal: land **2–3 free 2-week pilots** with Milton clinics, prove Norra books real
appointments, and collect testimonials. Lead with the demo — it sells itself.

---

## The offer (say this)

> "I'll set up an AI receptionist for your clinic, **free for two weeks**. It answers
> every call 24/7, books straight into your Jane (or calendar), and texts you a summary
> of each call. If it books even a few extra appointments, we keep going at a simple
> monthly rate. If not, no cost and no hard feelings."

Why it works: zero risk, obvious value (missed calls + no-shows), and you do all the setup.

## The demo (your closer)

1. Before/at the meeting, paste their **website URL** into the **Norra Demo Console**.
2. It loads their business into the demo agent.
3. Hand them the **demo number** and say: *"Call this and ask it anything — your hours,
   your prices, book an appointment."*
4. They hear their own receptionist. That's the moment they get it.

---

## Cold-call script (30 seconds)

> "Hi, this is Sashi — I run Norra, a local AI receptionist for Milton clinics. Quick
> question: roughly how many calls go to voicemail when your front desk is busy or after
> hours? … Right. I built something that answers every one of those, books the
> appointment into your system, and texts you a summary — and I can show you yours
> working in about two minutes. Could I set up a free two-week trial for your clinic?
> No cost, no commitment."

If yes → book a 15-min in-person or video demo.
If "send me info" → send the follow-up email + the website link.
